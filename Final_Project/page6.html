About Me


If you can't tell aready, I'm a HUGE nerd. I had a hard time writing stories because I had no guide to guide me through it and no idea where to start. So I thought why not help others that might be going through the same thing? I'm a tea lover, I enjoy listening to and producing music (I play the guiter, ukelele, and the piano). I occasionally do art, but I still love it intensely. I'm part of too many fandoms to count. They include Marvel and DC (Wonder Woman is my everything) book fandoms which I'm not going to list because that would take forever, and a lover of indie pop. I hope this website would help you in someway, getting you going along on your journey, or inspiring you to start one.

-Leyna
