Writing

Creating your story

After you know what it is you are wriing about, it's time to start drafting! This page will sectioned into different parts, like world building, characters, and plot planning.



Drafting/Outlining

In my own personal opinion, I absolutely HATE outlining. If you like it, that's benificial to you, but if you are like me this is your lucky day. Everyone knows what outlining is, but even before that, you should start with character profiles. But when you start outlining, never worry about being specific. Leave room to inovate, so you don't get bored writing your story because you already know what is going to happen. Discover the story along with the reader. And if you just hate outlining in general, create a first draft. This way you are surprised all the time, and have more to work off on when you go to do your other drafts. And yes, you will have MANY different drafts.



Character Development

Going back to character profiles, these are some pointers to what the profile should be like. What's their favorite hobby, what is their horoscope, what is their favorite Taylor Swift song? It may sound silly, but this really helps you understand and know your characters as if they are your best friend. This creates round characters that are relatable and help you create and overall more well rounded story.
