Wolves are one of the most reasearched and well-known species to ever roam this Earth. They are close relatives to dogs and have many similar characteristics. They are very social animals and often travel in packs. Wolves can live in deserts, grasslands, forests, and tundras.


Wolves are considered cunning and strong creatures. In some cultures they represent royalty and strength, while in others they symbolize corruption and chaos. Another dominant characteristic of the wolf that is portrayed in mythology is its maternal instincts.
